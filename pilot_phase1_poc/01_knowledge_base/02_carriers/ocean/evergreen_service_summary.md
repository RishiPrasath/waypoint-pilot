## About Evergreen

Evergreen Marine (Singapore) Pte. Ltd. ("EMS"), a member of the Evergreen Group, is a Singapore-flag container shipping company headquartered in Singapore. Established in December 2008, EMS is responsible for developing new markets with shipping hubs in Singapore and neighboring countries.

EMS markets its global container services under the brand name "Evergreen Line", in collaboration with other Evergreen Group companies:
- Evergreen Marine Corp. (Taiwan) Ltd.
- Italia Marittima S.p.A.
- Evergreen Marine (UK) Ltd.
- Evergreen Marine (Hong Kong) Ltd.
- Evergreen Marine (Asia) Pte. Ltd.

Benefiting from Singapore's strategic location, EMS operates close to a wide range of ancillary services in shipping, international trade, and logistics.

2. **Global Network**: Part of Evergreen Line with 1.7+ million TEU global capacity
3. **Ocean Alliance**: Member of major shipping alliance for extensive coverage
4. **Digital-First**: Comprehensive e-commerce platform for online operations
5. **Container Variety**: Wide range of dry, reefer, and special containers
6. **Strategic Partnerships**: Joint venture with PSA Singapore for terminal operations
7. **Sustainability Focus**: "Nature Above All" commitment to eco-friendly shipping

## Booking Process

1. **Register**: Create account on e-commerce platform
2. **Search Schedules**: Find sailings via Sailing Schedules tool
3. **Get Rates**: Check tariffs and rates online
4. **Book Online**: Submit booking via e-Booking
5. **Submit Documentation**: Provide shipping instructions and B/L details
6. **VGM Submission**: File Verified Gross Mass electronically
7. **Track Shipment**: Monitor via Cargo Tracking

## Documentation Requirements

### Export from Singapore

- Commercial Invoice
- Packing List
- Bill of Lading / Sea Waybill

- Dangerous Goods Declaration (if applicable)
- Certificate of Origin (if required)

### Import to Singapore

- Bill of Lading
- Commercial Invoice
- Packing List
- Import Permit
