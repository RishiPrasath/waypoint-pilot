# ASEAN Tariff Finder Guide

## Key Terms and Abbreviations

| Abbreviation | Full Term | Context |
|:------------|:----------|:--------|
| **MFN** | Most Favoured Nation | Default (non-preferential) duty rate applied to WTO members |
| **ATIGA** | ASEAN Trade in Goods Agreement | FTA providing preferential tariffs within ASEAN |
| **RCEP** | Regional Comprehensive Economic Partnership | FTA covering ASEAN + 5 partners (China, Japan, Korea, Australia, NZ) |
| **ACFTA** | ASEAN-China FTA | Bilateral FTA with preferential rates for China-ASEAN trade |
| **AKFTA** | ASEAN-Korea FTA | Bilateral FTA with preferential rates for Korea-ASEAN trade |
| **AIFTA** | ASEAN-India FTA | Bilateral FTA with preferential rates for India-ASEAN trade |
| **HS** | Harmonized System | International 6-digit product classification for tariffs |
| **AHTN** | ASEAN Harmonized Tariff Nomenclature | ASEAN's 8-digit extension of the HS code |

## Overview

The ASEAN Tariff Finder is a dedicated website that provides information on tariff rates and rules of origin requirements applied by ASEAN Member States and ASEAN FTA Partners under relevant Free Trade Agreements.

**Website**: [tariff-finder.asean.org](https://tariff-finder.asean.org)

## Purpose

The Tariff Finder helps businesses:
- Look up preferential tariff rates under ATIGA and other FTAs
- Identify rules of origin requirements for specific products
- Compare tariff rates across different ASEAN Member States
- Determine which FTA offers the best preferential treatment

## How to Use the ASEAN Tariff Finder

### Step 1: Register for an Account (Required)
- Navigate to `tariff-finder.asean.org`
- Click Register and provide company details
- Receive activation email
- Click activation link to complete registration
- **Note**: Registration is FREE but mandatory

### Step 2: Login and Select Your Parameters
- **Exporting Country**: Select the origin country of goods
- **Importing Country**: Select the destination ASEAN Member State
- **Product**: Enter the HS code or product description

### Step 3: View Results
The tool displays:
- Applied MFN (Most Favoured Nation) tariff rate
- Preferential tariff rate under ATIGA
- Preferential rates under other applicable FTAs
- Rules of Origin requirements to qualify for preferential rates

### Step 4: Check Rules of Origin
Review the specific criteria your goods must meet to qualify for preferential treatment:
- Regional Value Content (RVC) requirements
- Change in Tariff Classification (CTC) rules
- Product-Specific Rules (PSRs)

## Related ASEAN Trade Tools

### 1. ASEAN Trade Repository (ATR)
- **URL**: [atr.asean.org](https://atr.asean.org)
- Access trade information from National Trade Repositories
- Contains: tariff nomenclature, MFN/preferential tariffs, rules of origin, NTMs, trade laws

### 2. ASEAN Single Window (ASW)
- **URL**: [asw.asean.org](https://asw.asean.org)
- Electronic exchange of border documents among ASEAN Member States
- Expedites cargo clearance

### 3. ASEAN-wide Self-Certification (AWSC)
- **URL**: [awsc.asean.org](https://awsc.asean.org)
- Allows certified exporters to self-certify origin status
- Implemented since 20 September 2020

### 4. ASEAN Customs Transit System (ACTS)
- **URL**: [acts.asean.org](https://acts.asean.org)
- Computerised customs transit management system
- One customs formality for cross-border goods movement

### 5. ASSIST (Solutions for Investments, Services and Trade)
- **URL**: [assist.asean.org](https://assist.asean.org)
- Complaint submission facility for ASEAN-based businesses

## Key Information Available

| Information Type | Description |
|------------------|-------------|
| Tariff Rates | MFN and preferential rates by HS code |
| Rules of Origin | Product-specific and general ROO requirements |
| FTA Coverage | Which agreements apply to your product |
| Documentation | Required certificates and forms |

## Tips for Using the Tariff Finder

1. **Use Correct HS Codes**: Ensure you have the accurate 6-digit or 8-digit HS code
2. **Compare FTAs**: Check multiple agreements to find the best rate
3. **Verify ROO**: Ensure your goods meet origin requirements before claiming preferences
4. **Check Updates**: Tariff schedules are updated periodically

## Current Tariff Status (as of 2025)

ASEAN is virtually tariff-free under ATIGA:
- **98.86%** of products have tariffs fully eliminated
- Remaining products are mostly sensitive agricultural items
