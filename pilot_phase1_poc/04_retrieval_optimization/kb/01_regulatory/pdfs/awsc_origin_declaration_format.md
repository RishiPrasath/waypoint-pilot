**Indicative Format of Original Origin Declaration**

This format serves as an indicative guide and the certified exporter is free to use any other format that contains the required information.

|Name of Products|HS in six digit or<br>AHTN Code|Origin<br>conferring<br>criterion|FOB value when the<br>regional value content<br>origin criterion is used|Quantity of goods|Trademark, if<br>applicable|
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
|<br> <br>|<br> <br>|<br> <br>|<br> <br>|<br> <br>|<br> <br>|

The exporter of the product(s) covered by this document (Certified Exporter Authorisation code: 0000/XXXX) declares that, except where otherwise
clearly indicated, the product(s) satisfy the Rules of Origin to be considered as originating goods under ATIGA (ASEAN country of origin: .........).

**Authorised signature over printed/stamped**
**name of the signatory**
..

|planatory Note: For purposes of origin conferring criterion:|Col2|
|---|---|
|(a) Goods wholly obtained or produced in the exporting Member<br>State satisfying article 27 (Wholly Obtained) of the ATGA|"WO"|
|(b) Goods satisfying Article 28 (Non-Wholly Obtained) of the<br>ATGA<br>• <br>Regional Value Content<br> <br>• <br>Change in Tariff Classification<br> <br>• <br>Specific Processes<br> <br>• <br>Combination Criteria|<br> <br>Percentage of Regional Value Content, example "40%"<br> <br>The actual CTC rule, example "CC" or "CTH" or "CTSH"<br> <br>"SP"<br> <br>The actual combination criterion, example "CTSH+35%"<br>|
|(c) Goods satisfying paragraph 2 of Article 30 (Partial<br>Cumulation) of the ATIGA|"PC x%", where x would be the percentage of Regional Value<br>Content of less than 40%, example "PC 25%"|

2. The Certified Exporter is free to include additional information that he/she deems necessary.
