**Implementing Guidelines for the Product-Specific Rules**

**of the ASEAN Trade in Goods Agreement**

**0.** **Explanatory Note**

The Implementing Guidelines (hereinafter referred to as "the IGs") have been developed with the
intention of facilitating the understanding of the Product-Specific Rules (hereinafter referred to as
"the PSRs") of the ASEAN Trade in Goods Agreement (hereinafter referred to as "the
Agreement"). The IGs do not form part of the Agreement and in case there is any conflict between
the IGs and the Agreement, the provisions in the Agreement will prevail.

The IGs is treated as a living document that may be modified through revision and agreement by
the Sub-Committee on ATIGA Rules of Origin (SC-AROO), as necessary.

**1.** **Implementation of the Break-down Rules**
