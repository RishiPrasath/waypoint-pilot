When it comes to
delivering perishable

conditions and stringent
handling methods to
keep your goods fresh.

THRUFRESH is a
Singapore Airlines Cargo
service that brings together
the latest technology with
the highest level of care,
ensuring your cargo stays
fresh, even when it's half
the world away.

#### GLOBAL CONNECTIVITY

Our extensive flight
network means you
can get your perishable
cargo delivered to more
than 100 cities around
the world on time.

Our staff are trained to
handle your perishable cargo
with the highest level of care.
From monitoring conditions

perishable cargo with
customised solutions,
we are committed to
ensuring your shipment
arrives at its best.

Swift ramp transfers,
priority uplifts and
on-tarmac solutions

from origin to destination.
