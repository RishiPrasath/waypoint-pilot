## 2. Scope of Services

### 2.1 Services Offered

The Company provides the following freight forwarding services:

| Service Category | Description |
|------------------|-------------|
| **Sea Freight** | FCL, LCL, breakbulk, project cargo |
| **Air Freight** | General cargo, express, charter |
| **Customs Brokerage** | Import/export declaration, permits |
| **Documentation** | Bill of lading, certificates of origin, letters of credit |
| **Warehousing** | Storage, inventory management, distribution |
| **Value-Added Services** | Packing, labeling, cargo insurance |

### 2.2 Service Limitations

The Company acts as an **agent** and not as a carrier. The Company:

- Does not own or operate vessels, aircraft, or vehicles
- Arranges transportation through third-party carriers
- Is not liable for carrier actions or omissions
- Reserves the right to decline shipments at its discretion

### 2.3 Door-to-Door Service Definition

**Is customs clearance included in door-to-door service?**

**Yes, customs clearance is included as standard** in our door-to-door service.

| Service Component | Door-to-Door | Port-to-Port |
|-------------------|--------------|--------------|
| Collection from shipper | Included | Not included |
| Export customs clearance | Included | Not included |
| Ocean/air freight | Included | Included |
| **Import customs clearance** | **Included** | Not included |
| Destination delivery | Included | Not included |

**What's NOT included** (payable separately):
- Duties and taxes (always consignee's account unless DDP)
- Cargo insurance (available as add-on)
- Special permits/licenses (we handle application, fees extra)
- Storage beyond free time
- Inspection fees if cargo examined

**Note**: Under DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) terms, duties and taxes are also included in our service.

### 2.4 Import Permit Services

**Do you handle import permit applications?**

**Yes, we handle import permit applications** on your behalf as part of our customs brokerage services.

| Permit Type | We Handle | Requirements |
|-------------|-----------|--------------|
| Standard import permits | Yes | Complete shipping documents |
| Controlled goods permits | Yes | Customer's license required |
| Food/health permits (SFA) | Yes | Product registration needed |
| Transhipment permits | Yes | Routing details required |

**What We Do**:
- Apply via TradeNet on your behalf
- Track permit status
- Coordinate with relevant authorities (Singapore Customs, SFA, HSA, etc.)
- Handle permit amendments if needed

**Processing Times**:
| Permit Type | Standard Processing |
|-------------|---------------------|
| Standard import permit | Same day (if submitted by 2 PM) |
| Controlled goods | 1-3 working days |
| Food/health products | 3-5 working days |

**Fees**: Permit application fee + government fees (varies by permit type)

**Note**: For controlled goods, customer must hold the relevant license. We cannot apply for licenses on your behalf.

## 4. Booking and Cancellation

### 4.1 Booking Confirmation

A booking is confirmed when:

1. Customer submits complete booking request
2. Company issues booking confirmation number
3. Space is confirmed with carrier

### 4.2 Required Documentation

For booking confirmation, customer must provide:

| Document | Deadline |
|----------|----------|
| Commercial invoice | 3 working days before ETD |
| Packing list | 3 working days before ETD |
| Shipping instructions | 3 working days before ETD |
| Customs permits | 2 working days before ETD |
| Dangerous goods declaration | 5 working days before ETD |

### 4.3 Cancellation Policy

| Cancellation Timing | Charge |
|---------------------|--------|
| More than 7 days before ETD | No charge |
| 3-7 days before ETD | 25% of freight |
| Less than 3 days before ETD | 50% of freight |
| After cargo receipt | 100% of freight |

### 4.4 Amendments

| Amendment Type | Fee |
|----------------|-----|
| Consignee change | SGD 50 |
| Destination change | Re-quote required |
| Weight/volume adjustment | Rate recalculation |
| B/L amendment after issue | SGD 100 + carrier fee |

## 6. Liability and Insurance

### 6.1 Liability Limitations

The Company's liability is limited to:

| Service | Maximum Liability |
|---------|-------------------|
| Sea freight | 2 SDR per kg (Hague-Visby Rules) |
| Air freight | 22 SDR per kg (Montreal Convention) |
| Warehousing | SGD 100 per package |
| Customs brokerage | SGD 500 per declaration |

### 6.2 Exclusions from Liability

The Company is not liable for loss or damage caused by:

- Acts of God, war, terrorism
- Government actions, seizure, quarantine
- Customer's acts or omissions
- Inherent vice of goods
- Insufficient or improper packing
- Delays beyond Company's control
- Electrical or mechanical derangement of reefer

### 6.3 Cargo Insurance

**Recommendation**: Customers are strongly advised to obtain cargo insurance.

The Company can arrange marine cargo insurance:

| Coverage | Premium (approx.) |
|----------|-------------------|
| ICC(A) All Risks | 0.15-0.25% of CIF value |
| ICC(B) Named Perils | 0.10-0.15% of CIF value |
| ICC(C) Basic | 0.08-0.12% of CIF value |

Minimum premium: SGD 50 per shipment
