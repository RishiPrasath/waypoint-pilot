## About Cathay Cargo

Cathay Cargo is the air freight division of Cathay Pacific Airways Limited, one of Asia's leading airlines. With an extensive freighter and passenger aircraft network, Cathay Cargo provides comprehensive air cargo solutions connecting major markets worldwide through their Hong Kong hub.

The company operates the Cathay Cargo Terminal and works with subsidiary Air Hong Kong to provide extensive regional coverage, particularly in the Greater Bay Area (GBA) including specialized services like the Air-Land Fresh Lane.

## Booking Process

1. **Register**: Create account on Cathay Cargo portal
2. **Click & Ship**: Use online booking platform
3. **Check Schedules**: Verify flight availability
4. **Check Loadability**: Confirm flight can accommodate cargo
5. **Submit Documentation**: Complete eAWB and required documents
6. **Deliver Cargo**: Drop at SATS Airfreight Terminal 5
7. **Track Shipment**: Monitor via Track and Trace

## Documentation Requirements

### Export from Singapore

- Air Waybill (AWB) / Electronic AWB (eAWB)
- Commercial Invoice
- Packing List
- Shipper's Letter of Instruction
- Dangerous Goods Declaration (if applicable)
- CITES Permit (for live animals, if applicable)
- Shipper's Declaration for Lithium Batteries (if applicable)
- Certificate of Origin (if required)

### Import to Singapore

- Air Waybill
- Commercial Invoice
- Packing List
- Import Permit (via TradeNet)
- Certificate of Origin (for preferential tariffs)
- Product-specific permits (if applicable)
