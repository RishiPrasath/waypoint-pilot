## About SIA Cargo

Singapore Airlines has been in the cargo business for over 70 years - since they first flew the Airspeed Consuls in 1947. Singapore Airlines Cargo, a division within Singapore Airlines, operates a dedicated freighter fleet and complements it with bellyhold capacity on passenger flights operated by Singapore Airlines and Scoot.

With their extensive global network, SIA Cargo carries shipments to over 100 cities on freighters and in the cargo hold of passenger aircraft. Trucking services are also available to selected destinations.

## Booking Process

1. **Register**: Create account on SIA Cargo member portal
2. **Get Quote**: Contact sales team or use online tools
3. **Book Space**: Reserve cargo space based on flight schedules
4. **Prepare Documentation**: Complete AWB and required documents
5. **Deliver Cargo**: Drop cargo at SATS Airfreight Terminal
6. **Track Shipment**: Monitor via Track & Trace
7. **Receive Cargo**: Collect at destination

## Documentation Requirements

### Export from Singapore

- Air Waybill (AWB) / Electronic AWB (eAWB)
- Commercial Invoice
- Packing List
- Shipper's Letter of Instruction
- Dangerous Goods Declaration (if applicable)
- CITES Permit (for live animals, if applicable)
- Certificate of Origin (if required)

### Import to Singapore

- Air Waybill
- Commercial Invoice
- Packing List
- Import Permit (via TradeNet)
- Certificate of Origin (for preferential tariffs)
- Product-specific permits (if applicable)
