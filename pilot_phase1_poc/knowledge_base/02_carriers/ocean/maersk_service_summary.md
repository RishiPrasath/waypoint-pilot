## About Maersk

Maersk Singapore Pte. Ltd. has been connecting Singapore's businesses to key markets around the world since 1929, via a comprehensive road and rail network that joins seamlessly with their global ocean fleet. Ships call at Singapore port on East-West, North-South, and intra-Asia networks.

Maersk is the world's largest integrated container shipping company, offering end-to-end logistics solutions including ocean freight, inland transportation, warehousing, and customs services.

## Services Offered

### Ocean Freight Services

- **FCL (Full Container Load)** - Dedicated container shipments
- **LCL (Less than Container Load)** - Consolidated cargo options
- **Reefer Services** - Temperature-controlled shipping
- **Special Cargo** - Out-of-gauge and project cargo
- **Dangerous Goods** - IMO-certified handling

## Booking Process

1. **Get a Quote**: Use "Find a Price" tool or contact sales team
2. **Book Online**: Submit booking via Maersk.com or customer portal
3. **Submit Documentation**: Provide shipping instructions and cargo details
4. **VGM Submission**: Submit Verified Gross Mass before cutoff
5. **Cargo Delivery**: Deliver cargo to terminal before cutoff
6. **Track Shipment**: Monitor via online tracking
7. **Receive Cargo**: Collect at destination with documentation

## Documentation Requirements

### Export from Singapore

- Commercial Invoice
- Packing List
- Bill of Lading / Sea Waybill
- VGM Declaration (SOLAS requirement)
- Dangerous Goods Declaration (if applicable)
- Certificate of Origin (if required)

### Import to Singapore

- Bill of Lading
- Commercial Invoice
- Packing List
- Import Permit
- Certificate of Origin (for preferential tariffs)
