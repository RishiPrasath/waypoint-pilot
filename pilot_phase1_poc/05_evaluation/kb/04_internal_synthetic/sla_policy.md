## 5. Delivery SLAs

### 5.1 Standard Delivery Commitments

**What is our standard delivery SLA for Singapore?**

| Destination | Service Type | Delivery SLA | Notes |
|-------------|--------------|--------------|-------|
| **Singapore (local)** | Door-to-door | 1-2 working days from arrival | After customs clearance |
| **Singapore (local)** | Port pickup | Same day (if cleared by 2 PM) | Customer collects |
| **Malaysia (Peninsular)** | Door-to-door | 3-5 working days | Cross-border clearance |
| **Malaysia (East)** | Door-to-door | 5-7 working days | Additional transshipment |
| **ASEAN (other)** | Door-to-door | 5-10 working days | Varies by destination |

### 5.2 SLA Calculation

**Delivery SLA starts**: After cargo arrives at destination port/airport AND customs clearance is completed

**Excluded from SLA calculation**:
- Customs inspection delays
- Permit application delays
- Customer-caused delays (incomplete documents, unavailable for delivery)
- Force majeure events
- Public holidays

### 5.3 Expedited Delivery Options

| Service | SLA | Premium |
|---------|-----|---------|
| Express delivery (Singapore) | Same-day (if cleared by 12 PM) | +30% |
| Priority clearance | 4-hour customs processing | +S$150 |
| Weekend delivery | Saturday delivery available | +50% |

## 7. Issue Resolution SLAs

### 7.1 Issue Priority Classification

| Priority | Definition | Examples |
|----------|------------|----------|
| **P1 - Critical** | Service completely disrupted | Shipment stuck, cargo at risk |
| **P2 - High** | Major impact on operations | Documentation errors, delays |
| **P3 - Medium** | Moderate impact | Billing discrepancies, minor delays |
| **P4 - Low** | Minimal impact | Information requests, suggestions |

### 7.2 Resolution Timeframes

| Priority | First Response | Status Update | Resolution Target |
|----------|---------------|---------------|-------------------|
| **P1** | 15 minutes | Every 1 hour | 4 hours |
| **P2** | 30 minutes | Every 2 hours | 8 hours |
| **P3** | 2 hours | Every 4 hours | 24 hours |
| **P4** | 4 hours | Daily | 72 hours |

### 7.3 Escalation Triggers

Auto-escalation when SLA is at risk:

| Time Remaining | Escalation Level |
|----------------|------------------|
| 50% of SLA | Team Lead notified |
| 25% of SLA | Manager notified |
| SLA breach | Director notified |
| 2x SLA | VP/MD notified |

## 14. Exclusions

SLA commitments do not apply to:

- Force majeure events
- Carrier delays beyond our control
- Regulatory holds or inspections
- Customer-caused delays
- Incomplete or incorrect information from customer
- Public holidays
- System maintenance (with prior notice)
