**4 STATUS OF TRADE LIBERALIZATION:**

The implementation of ASEAN Member States (AMS) of their ATIGA trade commitments has resulted
to the elimination of tariffs for 98.6% of the total number of tariff lines in 2020, with 99.3% of the
tariff lines of ASEAN-6 and 97.7% of the tariff lines of CLMV carrying 0% ATIGA duty rate.
