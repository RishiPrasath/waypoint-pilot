**MATRIX OF MINOR DISCREPANCY ISSUE OF ASEAN MEMBER STATES**

**ON RULES OF ORIGIN***

|No|Minor Discrepancy Issue|
|---|---|
|1|Spelling and/or typing errors in the Proof of Origin (POO).|
|2|Size of tick marks and execution (either manual or typewritten), including<br>crossed instead of ticked.|
|3|Slight discrepancies in the authorized signature in the Certificate of Origin<br>(Form D) and/or Origin Declaration (OD) as compared to the authorized list of<br>signatories.|
|4|Different units of measurements stated in the POO and supporting documents<br>such as invoices/ packing list and supporting documents.|
|5|Minor differences in A4 paper size of the Certificate of Origin (Form D).|
|6|Minor discrepancies in ink colour (black or blue) of the text on the<br>Certificate of Origin (Form D).|
|7|Slight differences in the description of goods in the POO as compared to the<br>supporting documents.|
|8|Slight differences in the description of goods in the ASEAN-wide Self-<br>Certification (AWSC) Certified Exporters (CE) Database and in the Origin<br>Declaration (OD) provided that the keywords of the description of the<br>registered goods are reflected in the description of goods in the OD.|

- As of the 36 [th] Sub-Committee on ATIGA Rules of Origin (SC-AROO) Meeting, 2123 June 2021, via Video Conference Meeting.
