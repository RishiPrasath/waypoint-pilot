**Import Demurrage & Detention Calculator**

This allows you to calculate and check the number of free time days for the shipment to ensure no extra cost is

incurred upon empty return.

*Does not include calculation of port storage charges after 72 hours upon arrival - SG

**Guidelines for Online D&D Calculator and Purchasing Free Time Extension on Maersk.com:**

1. [Log-in to Maersk.com to view the Hub Dashboard.](https://www.maersk.com/hub/)

2. In **Import Demurrage & Detention widget**, enter the shipment number and click on **View details** .

3. Select **Detention** to view combined demurrage and detention last free date, and select return date to

calculate charges by selecting your **Empty return date** .

4. Once desired number of free days have been determined, ensure to check the offer acceptance box and

click on "Purchase detention days".

5. A green banner confirmation message will appear, and invoicing will be done accordingly.
