## About ONE

Ocean Network Express (ONE) is one of the world's largest liner shipping companies. Operating more than 260 vessels, ONE offers an expeditious and reliable international network of over 165 services to 120 countries and beyond. The company has a fleet size of approximately 2.0 million TEU and a global presence with more than 8,000 employees across over 50 countries.

ONE's slogan "As ONE, We Can" encapsulates their belief in teamwork - driving them to deliver quality and innovation to rise above any challenge.

## Booking Process

1. **Get a Quote**: Use ONE QUOTE tool or contact sales
2. **Book Online**: Submit booking via ONE eCommerce platform
3. **Submit Documentation**: Provide shipping instructions
4. **VGM Submission**: Submit Verified Gross Mass before cutoff
5. **Cargo Delivery**: Deliver cargo to terminal before cutoff
6. **Track Shipment**: Monitor via online tracking or mobile app
7. **Documentation**: Use ONE eBL for electronic bill of lading
